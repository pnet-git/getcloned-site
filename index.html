Questions

We know what you're thinking.

Will the clone actually sound and look like me?
Yes — that's the core promise. We use Professional Voice Cloning (not basic AI tools) and a Digital Twin avatar trained specifically on your voice, face, and delivery. Our standard: if you listen to the output without knowing it's AI, it should pass as you. We test every clone before going live.
Is this ethical? Will my patients or clients lose trust?
The knowledge, expertise, and messaging is 100% yours — we don't fabricate your views or make up information. You review and approve every script before it's produced. Think of it like a ghostwriter who writes in your voice, or a teleprompter you never have to stand in front of. The substance is real. The bottleneck (filming) is removed.
How much of my time does this actually require?
Minimal. For script approval: 15–20 minutes per batch (we send you batches, not one at a time). For strategy review: a monthly 30-minute check-in. That's it. You don't film, you don't edit, you don't upload. We handle everything else.
I already have an editor. How is this different?
An editor needs raw footage from you — which means you still have to film. Get Cloned removes that dependency entirely. No filming means no shoot days, no setup, no performance anxiety, no camera. Your editor can't solve the shooting bottleneck. We can.
How is this different from generic AI content tools?
Generic AI tools generate faceless, voiceless content that looks automated and impersonal. Get Cloned creates content that looks, sounds, and feels like YOU — with your face, your voice, your expert positioning, your niche topics. It's a done-for-you service, not a tool you operate yourself.
What if I don't convert into a full DFY client?
No problem. We offer a community-based implementation program where you get access to our full cloning system, SOPs, and support to build your own content engine. You get the same knowledge at a lower investment and build it yourself with our guidance.
Do I retain control of the content?
Completely. Every script is approved by you before production. Every video is reviewed by you before publishing. Nothing goes live without your explicit sign-off. You remain the expert, the authority, and the decision-maker at every step.
