The knowledge, expertise, and messaging is 100% yours — we don't fabricate your views or make up information. You review and approve every script before it's produced. Think of it like a ghostwriter who writes in your voice. The substance is real. The bottleneck (filming) is removed.

Minimal. Script approval: 15–20 minutes per batch. Monthly strategy review: 30 minutes. That's it. You don't film, edit, or upload. We handle everything else.

An editor needs raw footage — which means you still have to film. Get Cloned removes that dependency entirely. No filming means no shoot days, no setup, no camera anxiety. Your editor can't solve the shooting bottleneck. We can.

Generic AI tools generate faceless, voiceless content that looks automated. Get Cloned creates content that looks, sounds, and feels like YOU — with your face, your voice, your expert positioning, your niche topics. It's a done-for-you service, not a tool you operate yourself.
Do I retain full control of the content?
Completely. Every script is approved by you before production. Every video is reviewed before publishing. Nothing goes live without your explicit sign-off. You remain the expert, the authority, and the decision-maker at every step.
